## Quick Start

### Option 1: Guided Interview (Recommended for First-Time Users)
1. Launch the application
2. Click **"üöÄ Start Tax Interview"** in the sidebar
3. Answer the interview questions by navigating with **"‚Üê Back"** and **"Next ‚Üí"** buttons

6. Confirm or modify your form selection

4. Use the search box to find specific forms

1. After completing the interview and selecting forms
2. You can return to form selection anytime
3. Click **"üìã Skip to Tax Forms"** to modify your form selection
4. Add or remove forms as needed

### Tax Forms Selection Page

#### Understanding Form Information

Each form displays:
```
‚òë Form Name (Checkbox)
  Description and details
  Category: Estimated Time
```

Example:
```
‚òë Schedule A
  Itemized Deductions instead of standard deduction
  Category: Deductions | Estimated: 30 minutes
```

#### Form Selection

**Required Forms**
- Form 1040 is required for all returns
- Required forms are always checked and cannot be unchecked
- They appear with an asterisk (*) in the form name

**Optional Forms**
- Check the box to include a form
- Uncheck to exclude it from your return
- You can change your selection anytime

**Form Information**
- **Name & Number**: Official tax form identifier
- **Description**: What the form is used for
- **Estimated Time**: How long to complete (in minutes)
- **Category**: Type of form (Deductions, Income, etc.)

#### Using Search and Filter

**Quick Search**
```
1. Type in the search box
2. Forms filter in real-time
3. Results organized by category
4. Clear search to see all forms again
```

**Search Examples**
- Search "Schedule" ‚Üí Shows all schedules
- Search "income" ‚Üí Shows all income-related forms
- Search "1040" ‚Üí Shows Form 1040 and related forms
- Search "credit" ‚Üí Shows all tax credit forms

#### Form Organization by Category

**Core**
- Form 1040: Main income tax return (Required)

**Deductions**
- Schedule A: Itemized deductions

**Income**
- Schedule B: Interest and dividends
- Schedule C: Self-employment income
- Schedule E: Rental and partnership income

**Investments**
- Schedule D: Capital gains and losses
- Form 8949: Detailed securities transactions

**Credits**
- Schedule EIC: Earned Income Tax Credit
- Form 2441: Child and dependent care expenses
- Form 3468: Investment credits
- Form 5695: Residential energy credits

**Foreign**
- Form 1116: Foreign tax credit

**Employment**
- Schedule H: Household employment taxes

**Gifts & Estates**
- Form 3520: Gift tax return

**Cryptocurrency**
- Form 8801: Cryptocurrency transactions

#### Action Buttons

**Select All**
- Selects all available forms
- Keep required forms always selected
- Useful if you want to complete all forms

**Clear All**
- Deselects all optional forms
- Keeps required forms (Form 1040) selected
- Start fresh if needed

**Continue**
- Proceeds with selected forms
- Validates that Form 1040 is selected
- Takes you to form completion pages

#### Tips for Form Selection

1. **Start with Recommendations**: If coming from interview, recommended forms are pre-selected
2. **Review Descriptions**: Read each form's description to understand if you need it
3. **Check Time Estimates**: Consider total time for completing all selected forms
4. **Be Thorough**: Include all forms that apply to your situation
5. **Modify Anytime**: You can return to this page to add/remove forms

## Troubleshooting

### Interview Issues

**Q: Can I go back and change my answer?**
A: Yes! Click the "‚Üê Back" button to return to previous questions. Your answers are saved.

**Q: What if I skip some questions?**
A: Questions marked as required must be answered. Optional questions show their requirement status.

**Q: Can I restart the interview?**
A: Yes! Click "üöÄ Start Tax Interview" again to start over.

**Q: Why are certain forms recommended?**
A: Forms are recommended based on your interview answers and the tax rules for your situation.

### Forms Selection Issues

**Q: Why is Form 1040 locked?**
A: Form 1040 is required for all U.S. individual tax returns.

**Q: Can I select forms not recommended?**
A: Yes! You can select any form from the list, even if not recommended.

**Q: How do I know if I need a form?**
A: Read the description and estimated time. If it applies to your situation, select it.

**Q: What if I select too many forms?**
A: You'll just have more forms to complete. You can return to forms selection to remove unnecessary ones.
