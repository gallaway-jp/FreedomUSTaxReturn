Deduction Help

Standard Deduction: A fixed amount set by the IRS that reduces your taxable income. For 2025, it's $14,600 for single filers and $29,200 for married filing jointly.

Itemized Deductions: You list specific expenses. Choose this only if your total itemized deductions exceed the standard deduction amount.

You can deduct medical expenses that exceed 7.5% of your adjusted gross income (AGI). This includes payments for doctors, hospitals, prescription drugs, and qualified long-term care services.
